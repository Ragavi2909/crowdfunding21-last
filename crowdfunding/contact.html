Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a campaign?

Starting a campaign is easy! Simply visit our Start Campaign page and fill out the form with your campaign details. Our team will review your submission within 24-48 hours.

What types of campaigns do you support?

We support environmental campaigns including renewable energy, water conservation, waste management, reforestation, wildlife protection, sustainable farming, and environmental education projects.

How can I donate to campaigns?

Browse our active campaigns and click on any campaign to view details. You can donate securely using credit/debit cards or PayPal. All donations are processed through secure payment gateways.

Is my donation secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing to protect all transactions. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

What happens if a campaign doesn't reach its goal?

We operate on an all-or-nothing funding model. If a campaign doesn't reach its goal, all donations are refunded to the backers. This ensures that projects have sufficient funding to be completed successfully.

How do I contact campaign creators?

Each campaign page has a comment section where you can ask questions directly to the campaign creators. You can also contact our support team if you need additional assistance.
